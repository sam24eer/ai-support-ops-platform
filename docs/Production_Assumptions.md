\# Production Assumptions \& Limits



\# Assumptions

\- Low to moderate ticket volume (SMB scale)

\- Stateless backend instances

\- Single-region deployment

\- Human review available for escalations



\# Current Limits

\- In-memory metrics (non-persistent)

\- Stubbed intent classifier

\- No rate limiting

\- No authentication layer



\# Rationale

These tradeoffs were intentional to focus on AI decision control,

observability, and operational design at MVP stage.
