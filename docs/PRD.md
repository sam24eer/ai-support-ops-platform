\# Product Requirements Document



\## Problem

Small and mid-sized businesses struggle with slow, manual customer support,

leading to poor response times, high costs, and customer churn.



\## Users

\- Support Agents

\- Support Managers

\- Business Owners



\## Goals

\- Reduce response time

\- Auto-resolve simple issues

\- Escalate complex cases safely

\- Provide operational visibility



\## Non-Goals

\- Voice support

\- Multilingual handling

\- Full CRM replacement



\## Success Metrics

\- Ticket deflection rate

\- Escalation rate

\- SLA breaches

\- Cost per ticket



\## MVP Scope

\- Web-based ticket intake

\- AI intent classification

\- Rule-based decision engine

\- Metrics and observability
